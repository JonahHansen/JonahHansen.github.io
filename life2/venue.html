Venue and Registration

  • The conference will take place at CosmoCaixa Barcelona (Carrer d'Isaac Newton, 26).
  • Access instructions:
    • The main entrance is on Carrer d’Isaac Newton 26. Follow the stairs or elevator one floor down, where you will find the registration area clearly signposted. It is also possible to access CosmoCaixa from the corner of Carrer dels Quatre Camins with Carrer del Cister, where you will arrive directly at the registration floor. After registration, directions will be provided to reach the main auditorium (Auditori).
