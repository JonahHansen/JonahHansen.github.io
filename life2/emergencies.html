Emergencies and Health Centers

The emergency number in Barcelona is 112. The public healthcare system in Catalonia is of very high quality and many health centers are distributed across Barcelona.

Public Health Centres

  • CAP Vallcarca - Sant Gervasi (Closest primary care center, weekdays, working hours only)
  • CUAP Gràcia (Closest 24hr emergency primary care center)
  • Hospital Universitari Vall d’Hebron (Public hospital with 24/7 emergency services)

Private Hospitals with 24/7 emergency service

  • Hospital Quirónsalud

For residents in the EU (outside Spain), it’s strongly advisable to carry with you the European Health Card, with which you can access easily and for free health centers and hospitals. Without it, you would still be seen by a doctor, but it’s possible that you would have to pay and then ask for a refund from your national healthcare system. For residents elsewhere, personal health insurance is essential.
